Arc

An arc or a fan if filled.

Special Properties

  • Fill (fill). If yes, the arc will be filled as a fan.
  • Start Angle (start_angle). The start angle of the arc in degree.
  • Total Angle (total_angle). The total angle of the arc in degree.
